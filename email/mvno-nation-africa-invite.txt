5 SESSIONS - ONE DAY - CAPE TOWN

The math, the graveyard, the winners, and the operating model
behind 250k active subscribers.

01. Why churn, not wholesale rate, decides profitability
    Drop monthly churn from 5% to 3% and LTV rises 67%.
    Retention compounds. Discounting does not.

02. Every price-led MVNO ended the same way
    9 case studies. US$2bn+ in investor capital burned.
    Combined investor return: near zero. Africa has its
    own graveyard.

03. The winners solved a specific problem for a defined audience
    Tesco Mobile, giffgaff, Lebara, Lycamobile, Consumer Cellular,
    FRiENDi. None won on price. Plenty of lessons for Africa.

04. Five pillars. One operating system.
    CX Differentiator, AI-first Ops, Digital-native architecture,
    Data-driven growth, Ecosystem partners.

05. Two South African MVNOs. One operating model.
    DStv Internet (250k active subscribers, 85.8% CSAT) and
    Boxer Telecom (92.31% CSAT, 45% BOT deflection). Both
    built and run from here.
